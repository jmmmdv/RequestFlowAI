# Pilot outreach kit

Copy-paste messages for the first RequestFlow AI pilot customers. Keep claims honest: pilot-ready
demo, rule-based triage, not a launched commercial product.

**Live demo:** https://request-flow-ai-steel.vercel.app  
**Code:** https://github.com/jmmmdv/RequestFlowAI

## Who to contact first

- Small agency owners drowning in client email/WhatsApp requests
- IT/support freelancers who need a simple request portal before Zendesk/Freshdesk
- Consultants who want clients to submit work in one place

## Discovery call questions

1. Where do client requests arrive today (email, WhatsApp, forms)?
2. What gets lost or delayed most often?
3. Who needs to see status — just your team or clients too?
4. Would a shareable request link be enough for the first month?
5. What would make you pay $29–79/month after the pilot?

## Pilot success criteria (log in PILOT-LOG.md)

- [ ] Client submitted a request through the public form
- [ ] Team saw it in the inbox with suggested priority
- [ ] Work item created and moved to Done
- [ ] Feedback captured (what confused them, what they loved)
