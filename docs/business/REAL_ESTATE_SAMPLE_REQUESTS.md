# Real estate sample requests

Twenty realistic examples for demos, pilot onboarding, and test submissions. Categories and priorities
align with RequestFlowAI’s **rule-based** classifier (`SALES`, `GENERAL`, `CHANGE_REQUEST`, `SUPPORT`,
`BILLING` + `CRITICAL`, `HIGH`, `MEDIUM`, `LOW`).

**Related:** [REAL_ESTATE_DEMO_SCRIPT.md](REAL_ESTATE_DEMO_SCRIPT.md)

---

## 1. Buyer lead — McLean budget

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Request title** | Buyer looking in McLean under USD 1.2M |
| **Requester type** | Buyer |
| **Example message** | Hi, we are relocating from Boston and want 4BR near McLean schools. Budget around USD 1.1–1.2M. Need to move by August. Can someone call us this week? |
| **Suggested category** | SALES |
| **Suggested priority** | MEDIUM |
| **Recommended next action** | Confirm budget, timing, and preferred neighborhoods; assign buyer agent and schedule intro call within 24 hours. |
