Copy this table into a new note, spreadsheet, or doc for each outreach sprint. One row per business
contacted or spoken with.

**Related:** [CUSTOMER_OUTREACH_MESSAGES.md](CUSTOMER_OUTREACH_MESSAGES.md) ·
[PILOT_TARGET_CUSTOMERS.md](PILOT_TARGET_CUSTOMERS.md) · [PILOT_VALIDATION_PLAN.md](PILOT_VALIDATION_PLAN.md)

## Tracking rules

1. **Talk to at least 10 businesses** before enabling paid AI or major new features.
2. **Aim for 3 pilot customers** who will route real requests for 30 days.
3. **Do not build custom features** for one customer unless they are paying or strongly likely to pay (verbal yes to paid plan + champion with authority).
4. **Track objections carefully** — recurring objections become product, pricing, or positioning fixes.
5. Log every touch (cold message, call, follow-up) with date and outcome.
6. Honest **No** is valuable data; do not chase free customization.

## Column guide

| Column | What to write |
|---|---|
| **Date** | First meaningful contact (message sent or call held) |
| **Business name** | Legal or trade name |
| **Industry** | From [PILOT_TARGET_CUSTOMERS.md](PILOT_TARGET_CUSTOMERS.md) |
| **Contact person** | Name + role (owner, office manager, etc.) |
| **Current request problem** | Their words — missed messages, no priority, chaos, etc. |
| **Monthly request volume estimate** | Rough number; OK if guess ("~50/week") |
| **Current tools** | Email, Google Voice, Jobber, spreadsheet, etc. |
| **Interested?** | Yes / No / Maybe — update after each touch |
| **Pilot price discussed** | Free, USD 99 one-time, USD 199/month, or none yet |
| **Follow-up date** | Next action on calendar |
| **Notes** | Objections, quotes, who decides, HIPAA/legal flags |
| **Decision** | Pilot / Nurture / Disqualify / Converted |
