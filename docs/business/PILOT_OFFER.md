# RequestFlowAI Early Access Pilot

A simple offer for the first real customers. Use this to set expectations before any paid AI or
heavy customization.

**Related:** [PILOT_VALIDATION_PLAN.md](PILOT_VALIDATION_PLAN.md) · [PRICING.md](../finance/PRICING.md)

---

## Pilot name

**RequestFlowAI Early Access Pilot**

---

## Duration

**30 days** from go-live (first production request submitted or intake link shared — pick one date
and confirm in email).

---

## Setup fee

**USD 0** for the first pilot customers.

---

## Pilot price options

| Option | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **A — Feedback pilot** | USD 0 | Strong feedback partner; testimonial or case study if it goes well |
| **B — Commitment pilot** | USD 99 one-time | Customer wants light skin in the game; still early-stage product |
| **C — Early paid pilot** | USD 199 / month | Customer wants priority attention and near-production usage |

### Recommendation for first 3 customers

Start with **Option A (free)** or **Option B (USD 99)** only.

- Free works when you need logos, quotes, and honest usage data.
- USD 99 filters serious buyers without enterprise procurement.
- **Defer Option C** until you have at least one successful free or USD 99 pilot and a stable
  onboarding checklist.

---

## What is included

- One organization workspace with admin dashboard access
- Public request intake link (slug-based portal)
- Rule-based classification, priority suggestion, and recommended next action
- Request status tracking and team visibility (within current product limits)
- Founder-led onboarding call (up to 30 minutes)
- Two check-in calls during the 30 days (15 minutes each)
- Email support during business hours (best-effort, founder-led)

---

## What is not included

- Paid OpenAI / LLM analysis (disabled by policy until post-pilot validation)
- Custom integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zapier beyond roadmap)
- SLA or 24/7 support
- White-label or on-premise deployment
- Unlimited seats or unlimited request volume beyond plan caps
- Legal review or custom DPA (use standard terms; escalate case-by-case)
- Data migration from legacy tools

---

## Customer responsibilities

1. **Share the intake link** with real clients or internal requesters — not demo data only.
2. **Assign one champion** who checks the dashboard at least weekly.
3. **Attend** onboarding and at least one check-in call.
4. **Give candid feedback** — what worked, what failed, what would make them pay.
5. **Tell you actual volume** — requests per week and who handles them.
6. **Respect fair use** — no spam, abuse, or sensitive data you would not put in a normal SaaS tool.

---

## Feedback expectations

By day 30, the customer agrees to:

- One **30-minute retrospective call** (or async written answers if they prefer)
- Answers to: usage frequency, time saved, missed requests avoided, missing features, fair price
- Permission to use **anonymous feedback** internally always; **name/logo** only with written approval

---

## Pilot success definition

The pilot is **successful** when:

- Real requests flowed through RequestFlow AI (not just test submissions)
- The champion used the dashboard without daily reminders from you
- You have a clear **continue at paid plan**, **extend pilot**, or **honest stop** decision
- You captured enough data to validate [pricing](../finance/PRICING.md) and [unit economics](../finance/UNIT_ECONOMICS.md)

At pilot end, offer conversion to **Starter (USD 29)**, **Growth (USD 79)**, or **Business (USD 199)**
per [PRICING.md](../finance/PRICING.md). Document any discount and end date in writing.
