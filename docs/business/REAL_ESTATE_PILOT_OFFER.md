# RequestFlowAI Real Estate Pilot

A simple, real estate–focused pilot offer for the first conversations with agents, small teams, and
broker offices. Not a contract — use as a conversation guide and follow up in email.

## Pilot name

**RequestFlowAI Real Estate Pilot**

---

## Duration

**30 days** from go-live (first day the public intake link is shared or first real request is submitted — agree on one date in writing).

---

## Setup fee

**USD 0** for first pilot customers.

---

## Recommended first pilot price

| Option | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **A — Feedback pilot** | USD 0 | Strong feedback partner; testimonial or quote if it goes well |
| **B — Commitment pilot** | USD 99 one-time | Customer wants light commitment; still early-stage product |
| **C — Early paid pilot** | USD 199 / month | Serious daily usage and priority founder attention |

### Recommendation for the first real estate pilot

Start with **Option A (free)** or **Option B (USD 99)** only if the customer agrees to **detailed
feedback** — weekly notes, exit interview, and permission to describe results anonymously if needed.

- **Free** is right when you need honest usage data and market vocabulary from a working agent.
- **USD 99** filters tire-kickers without enterprise procurement.
- **Defer Option C** until one successful free or USD 99 pilot completes and onboarding is repeatable.

---

## What is included

- One organization workspace with admin dashboard access
- Public request intake link (slug-based portal for buyer/seller/general inquiries)
- Rule-based classification, priority suggestion, internal summary, and recommended next action
- Request status tracking and team visibility (within current plan limits)
- Founder-led onboarding call (up to 30 minutes) — intake link placement and workflow fit
- Two check-in calls during the 30 days (15 minutes each)
- Email support during business hours (best-effort, founder-led)
- Help loading [sample requests](REAL_ESTATE_SAMPLE_REQUESTS.md) for practice submissions

---

## What is not included

- MLS, Zillow, or CRM two-way sync
- Automated SMS, WhatsApp, or email drip campaigns
- AI / OpenAI-powered summaries or reply drafting (disabled until validated)
- Custom branding beyond current product capabilities
- Transaction management, e-signature, or commission accounting
- Dedicated SLA, 24/7 support, or on-site training
- Legal review of brokerage or franchise compliance (customer owns compliance fit)

---

## Customer responsibilities

- Share where the intake link will live (website, email signature, open house QR, etc.)
- Route **real leads** or realistic test leads through the portal during the pilot
- Check the request inbox at least once per business day (or assign one owner)
- Respond to clients — RequestFlowAI organizes intake; it does not replace agent follow-up
- Attend kickoff and at least one check-in call
- Provide candid feedback — what helped, what did not, what is missing

---

## Feedback expectations

During the 30 days, the customer agrees to:

1. **Week 1** — Confirm intake link is live and at least 3 requests submitted
2. **Week 2** — 15-minute check-in: priority accuracy, workflow friction, missing features
3. **Week 4** — 20-minute exit call with structured notes:
   - Would you keep using this after the pilot?
   - What would you pay (if anything)?
   - What one feature would matter most next?
   - May we use an anonymous quote if results are positive?

---

## Success criteria

| Criterion | Directional target |
|---|---|
| Usage | 15+ requests through the portal (real or realistic test) |
| Engagement | Assigned owner checks inbox 4+ days per week |
| Response time | Measurable or perceived improvement in first reply |
| Missed leads | Fewer lost threads vs baseline (self-reported) |
| Decision | Clear continue / pause / stop with reasons |

Success is **validated learning**, not forced conversion.

## Suggested next step after pilot

| Outcome | Suggested path |
|---|---|

## Internal note

Do not oversell. A realtor who says “my CRM already does this” is useful data. Ask what their CRM
**does not** do at the moment a lead first arrives.
