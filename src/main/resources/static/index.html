PRICING FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel through the billing portal at any time. Your workspace keeps access through the end of the paid billing period.
Is this for small teams?
Yes. RequestFlow AI is built for small businesses, agencies, consultants, and IT/support teams that need one request inbox before enterprise tools make sense.
Do I need technical setup?
No. Sign up, finish workspace setup, and share your public request link. Most teams send their first client request the same day.
What happens after the free plan limit?
When you reach your work-item or assisted-plan limit, new items pause until you upgrade to Pro ($49/month) or remove older work. Your existing data stays safe.
